# Example

Calcuate the angle at which a road should be banked if the minumum velocity of a car on it should be 5$m/s$. The radius of banking is 50$m$ and the coefficient of static friction between the car and the road should be 0.25.

## Solution 

$$
v_{min} = \sqrt{
    \frac{rg(sin\theta - \mu _{s} cos\theta)}{cos\theta + \mu _{s} sin\theta}
} = \sqrt{
    \frac{70 \times 9.8(sin10^\circ - 0.3cos10)}{cos10^\circ + 0.3sin10}
}
$$
