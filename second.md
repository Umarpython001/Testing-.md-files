## Kinematics

The first equation of motion is:

$$
v = u + at
$$

The displacement equation:

$$
s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^{2}
$$

## Energy

Kinetic energy:

$$
E_k = \frac{1}{2}mv^2
$$

## Vector notation

A force vector can be written as:

$$
\vec{F} = m\vec{a}
$$

## Inline usage

If $a = 9.8\,\text{m s}^{-2}$ and $t = 3\,\text{s}$, then  
$v = at = 29.4\,\text{m s}^{-1}$.
